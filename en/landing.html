FAQ

Frequently asked.

How long does an audit take?
Between 15 and 30 seconds. The page is fetched, analyzed by AI across 8 dimensions, and the interactive report shows up. You can download a standalone HTML version.
What's the scoring based on?
15 years of CRO best practices: Hero (×2), CTA (×2), Copywriting (×1.5), 5 other dimensions equally weighted. Final mention: Weak <40, Average 40-60, Good 60-80, Excellent >80.
Can I test with my own API key?
Yes — and it's free on our side. Enable BYOK mode in the app with your Anthropic, OpenAI, Groq, xAI, Mistral or OpenRouter key. You pay your provider directly, no ConvertAudit credits used.
Is my data stored?
No. Audits are processed on the fly. No server-side storage. Your URLs and results live only in your browser.
Can I get a refund?
If the report fails to render due to a bug, yes. No refunds on successful audits — the AI cost is borne immediately on our side.
