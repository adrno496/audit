FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does an audit take?
Between 15 and 30 seconds. The page is fetched, analyzed by AI across 8 dimensions, and the interactive report shows up. You can download a standalone HTML version.
What's the scoring based on?
15 years of CRO best practices: Hero (×2), CTA (×2), Copywriting (×1.5), and 5 other dimensions equally weighted. Final mention: Weak <40, Average 40-60, Good 60-80, Excellent >80.
My site is in French — does it work?
Yes. You pick the report language (FR or EN) at audit time, independently of the language of the site being analyzed.
Can I test with my own API key?
Yes — and it's free on our side. Enable BYOK mode in the app (⚙ icon) with your Anthropic, OpenAI, Groq, xAI, Mistral or OpenRouter key. You pay your provider directly, no ConvertAudit credits used.
Is my data stored?
No. Audits are processed on the fly: the page is fetched, analyzed, the report is returned. No server-side storage in v1. Your URLs and results live only in your browser.
What about Cloudflare-protected sites or login walls?
Public pages (no login) work in 90-95% of cases. Cloudflare with strict JS challenges or pages behind auth aren't auditable — you can copy-paste the HTML manually instead (v2 feature).
Can I get a refund?
If the report fails to render due to a bug, yes (contact via Lemonsqueezy). No refunds on successful audits — the AI cost is borne immediately on our side.
