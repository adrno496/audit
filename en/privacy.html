2. Data collected

Data stored on our servers

No audit data is stored on our servers. Analysis runs on the fly: the page is fetched, sent to an AI provider (see §4), the report is returned, and the data only transits through our Edge Function's ephemeral memory.

Data stored in your browser (localStorage)

  • Credit counter (convertaudit_credits): a number tracking remaining audits
  • 1-hour audit cache (convertaudit_cache_v1): recent reports to avoid re-running identical audits. Auto-expires after 1 hour.
  • BYOK config (convertaudit_byok, optional): if you use your own API key, it's stored locally in your browser. It's never sent to our servers; in BYOK mode, it goes directly from browser to your chosen AI provider.

You can delete this data anytime via your browser's developer tools (DevTools → Application → Local Storage) or by clearing the site's cache.

Data collected at purchase (Lemonsqueezy)

Purchases are processed by Lemonsqueezy, our payment partner, who collects email, billing name and address (if applicable), payment information, country (for VAT / Sales Tax). ConvertAudit only receives your purchase email for delivery purposes.

3. Cookies

ConvertAudit uses no tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no Facebook / Google Ads pixels. No third-party analytics integrated.

5. Retention

  • Audits: not stored on our servers (zero retention). Browser cache expires after 1 hour.
  • Credits: kept until you clear your browser's localStorage. No server-side link.
  • Purchase email (Lemonsqueezy): kept per Lemonsqueezy's policy (typically 7 years for accounting compliance).

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under EU Regulation 2016/679 you have:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@convertaudit.app. We'll respond within one month.

You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (CNIL in France).

7. Security

  • HTTPS (TLS) connection enforced
  • API keys stored as Supabase secrets (never in source code or logs)
  • No prolonged storage of sensitive information server-side
  • In BYOK mode: your key stays in your browser and only transits to the provider you chose

8. Updates to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect service or legal changes. The "last updated" date is shown at the top. Substantial changes will be communicated via email (if you're a Lemonsqueezy customer) or via a banner on the site.
