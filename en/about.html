About

Conversion optimization, made simple.

ConvertAudit was born from a simple observation: most landing pages lose visitors on basic mistakes that an expert can spot in seconds.

Our mission

Make CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) expertise accessible to freelancers, SaaS makers and small agencies — those who don't have the budget for a senior consultant at $1000/day, nor the time to become CRO experts themselves.

Today's AI can analyze a landing page in 30 seconds with a rigor comparable to a seasoned human — for the price of a coffee. That's the promise behind ConvertAudit: a professional, instant audit, at €4.90.

How does ConvertAudit work?

We combine three ingredients:

  1. Page extraction: we pull the HTML, headings, CTAs and visible text from the URL you provide.
  2. Generative AI analysis: a state-of-the-art model (Claude Sonnet 4.6 by default) evaluates the page across 8 conversion dimensions inherited from 15 years of CRO best practices.
  3. Actionable synthesis: we turn raw analysis into a 0-100 score, prioritized action plan, and downloadable HTML report.

The 8 dimensions evaluated

Our framework is inspired by proven CRO methodologies (AIDA, PAS, jobs-to-be-done) applied to the landing page experience:

  • Hero — value proposition clarity, headline, above the fold
  • Copywriting — structure, benefits vs features, readability
  • Call-to-Action — wording, placement, friction
  • Social proof — testimonials, logos, numbers, certifications
  • Visual structure — hierarchy, sections, scannability
  • Trust signals — HTTPS, legal mentions, guarantees
  • Urgency / Scarcity — honest incentives to act
  • Mobile & speed — viewport, basic optimizations

Why not a human?

A senior CRO consultant remains irreplaceable for high-volume accounts. But for 90% of cases — a freelancer improving their lead capture page, a SaaS maker iterating on their homepage, an agency auditing 10 prospects — AI is more than enough and saves both time and money.

Our audits don't replace deep user research or A/B tests on large volumes. They're designed to quickly identify obvious frictions and provide a concrete plan to fix them.

Our stack

  • Frontend: native HTML / CSS / JavaScript — no framework, no build, opens with a double-click
  • Backend: Supabase Edge Functions (Deno) to relay AI calls and bypass CORS
  • AI: Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4.6 by default, with multi-provider support (OpenAI, Groq, Mistral, xAI, OpenRouter) in BYOK mode
  • Payment: Lemonsqueezy — €4.90 per audit, volume discounts, no subscription

BYOK mode: bring your own key

We've integrated a Bring Your Own Key mode that lets each user test ConvertAudit with their own API key (Anthropic, OpenAI, Groq, Mistral, xAI or OpenRouter). No ConvertAudit credit is consumed — you pay your provider directly, often less than $0.01 per audit.

It's our way of respecting users who want full control over their costs or who are already customers of these services.

Our commitments

  • No storage: audited URLs and reports are not kept on our servers
  • No tracking: no third-party analytics cookie, no pixel, no external script (apart from Lemonsqueezy at checkout)
  • No subscription: pay per audit, credits valid forever
  • Transparent code: most of the code is in your browser, inspectable via right-click → Inspect
