About

Conversion optimization, simplified.

ConvertAudit was born from a simple observation: most landing pages lose visitors on basic mistakes that an expert can spot in seconds.

Launched 2026Solo · independentMade in EU

Our mission

Make CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) expertise accessible to freelancers, SaaS makers and small agencies — those who don't have the budget for a senior consultant at $1,000/day, nor the time to become CRO experts themselves.

Today's AI can analyze a landing page in 30 seconds with a rigor comparable to a seasoned human — for the price of a coffee. That's the promise: a professional, instant audit at €4.90.

"You don't pay for expertise. You pay for the time it saves."

How does ConvertAudit work?

  1. Page extraction — pull HTML, headings, CTAs and visible text from the URL.
  2. Generative AI analysis — Claude Sonnet 4.6 by default evaluates 8 conversion dimensions.
  3. Actionable synthesis — 0-100 score, prioritized action plan, downloadable HTML report.

The 8 dimensions

  • Hero — value proposition clarity, headline, above the fold
  • Copywriting — structure, benefits vs features, readability
  • Call-to-Action — wording, placement, friction
  • Social proof — testimonials, logos, numbers, certifications
  • Visual structure — hierarchy, sections, scannability
  • Trust signals — HTTPS, legal mentions, guarantees
  • Urgency / Scarcity — honest incentives
  • Mobile & speed — viewport, basic optimizations

Why not a human?

A senior CRO consultant remains irreplaceable for high-volume accounts. But for 90% of cases, AI is enough and saves time and money.

Our angle — We target the 80% of weak points you can fix yourself in a few hours. For the remaining 20% (purchase psychology, segmentation, large-volume A/B tests), you still need humans.

Our stack

  • Frontend — native HTML/CSS/JS. Zero framework, zero build.
  • Backend — Supabase Edge Functions (Deno).
  • AI — Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4.6, with multi-provider BYOK support.
  • Payment — Lemonsqueezy. €4.90 per audit, no subscription.

BYOK mode

Bring Your Own Key lets each user test ConvertAudit with their own API key. No credit consumed — you pay your provider directly, often less than €0.01 per audit.

Our commitments

  • No storage — audited URLs and reports not kept on our servers.
  • No tracking — no analytics cookies, no pixels, no external scripts.
  • No subscription — pay per audit, credits valid forever.
  • Transparent code — most code runs in your browser, inspectable.
